What do you like in note-taking apps?
 - Different colours
 - Checkable lists, bullet points, numbered lists
 - Different font sizes, bolding (rich text)
 - Cuteness, prettyness, you can make banners
 - Dropdown menus, dividers with labels potentially
 - Background options (see OneNote)
 - Sticky notes on the page, adjustable sizing
 - Convert to the correct font automatically
 - Responsive, performant
 - Sorting by time, name, topic, etc.
 - Themes


 - Notion is too bulky on mobile, Apple Notes is clean
